Dear Human, thank you for your interest in this call.
he Cookie Collective is calling for contributions for a collective visual release on the theme DIVAGATION (a word with a wide polysemic meaning ;)
This project is actually about an audiovisual collective compilation, and we'll then ask some other humans to compose a piece of music for your images. Final work is due on 03/03/24.
The theme is "DIVAGATION", and here are the numerous definitions:
The act of wandering here and there, out of place.
The action of a mind wandering, wandering at the whim of its reverie.
The contravention of letting animals wander or abandoning them on the roads.
Displacement, total or partial, permanent or temporary, of a river bed.
Synonyms: elucubration - extravagance - incoherence - rambling - reverie - daydream.
Use any technique you like that isn't too conventional [digital and/or analog]. We'd love to see your handicrafts, your collages, your personal proposals...
Requested:

- EVERYTHING that is not forbidden

Forbidden:

- AI (yup)

- VR

- All forms of discrimination and hate speech.

Minimum format (unless technically required) :

- video length: from 33 seconds to 450 seconds

- ratio: 4/3 up to 16/9

- format: mp4 - h264

- resolution: 4p to 4k

- FPS: 24 to 30

The final result of this project will be an audio-visual compilation, a git repository, a fanzine, a dvd, and a release party.

The compilation will be available online.

The git repository will compile the technical and personal information you wish to pass on.

The fanzine will contain all the information + a USB stick with your creations. It will be printed in 100 copies. One copy will be sent if you are in.
The release party will take place in Paris in June
